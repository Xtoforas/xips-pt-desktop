# Phase 6: Settings and Diagnostics Cleanup

## Goal

Reduce top-level clutter by moving routine operational work out of `Settings` while preserving support depth in `Diagnostics`.

## Scope

- [ ] Move routine operational controls out of `Settings` when they belong on `Today` or `Automation`.
- [ ] Keep server profile and desktop behavior controls in `Settings`.
- [ ] Simplify `Diagnostics` visually while preserving current troubleshooting depth.
- [ ] Preserve access to diagnostic export and app-data actions.

## Deliverables

- Slimmer `Settings` page focused on configuration.
- Reframed `Diagnostics` page with clearer support-only positioning.
- Updated copy so support actions and normal workflow actions are not mixed together.

## Dependencies

- Depends on [Phase 1](phase-1-information-architecture-and-shell.md).
- Should be finalized after operational responsibilities are redistributed in [Phase 3](phase-3-today-page-redesign.md) and [Phase 5](phase-5-automation-workflow-redesign.md).

## Validation

- [ ] Verify routine user actions no longer require visits to `Settings`.
- [ ] Verify diagnostic export and app-data access remain intact.
- [ ] Verify troubleshooting information remains discoverable when needed.
